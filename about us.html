Meet Jus - Founder and Creative Force

About Jusbakery

Meet Jus, the creative force and founder of Jusbakery, a vibrant hub of culinary artistry and passion located in the heart of Kyaliwajjala. Our journey is colored with the sweet aroma of success, stemming from the humble roots of a chips business.

Jusbakery is not just a place that makes delicious cakes and excels in event planning; it's a nurturing ground for aspiring bakers. Jus has shared her knowledge with over 30 students, transforming them into skilled bakers who are now part of our extended family.

Explore the enchanting journey of Jusbakery, where every creation is a celebration of shared dreams and culinary delights. The dedication to quality and taste sets us apart, making every bite a delightful experience. Join us on this delectable journey, where the magic of quality intertwines with the sweetness of dreams.

At Jusbakery, we not only create mouthwatering cakes and plan memorable events, but we also offer lessons to all bake dreamers. Whether you choose our day classes or immersive boarding experience, the doors to learning are always wide open. Our teaching philosophy is grounded in hands-on experience, ensuring that every student not only acquires baking skills but also discovers the artistry and passion behind the craft.
